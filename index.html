AksharDham Temple

Akshardham is a Hindu temple complex in Delhi, India. Also referred to as Delhi Akshardham or Swaminarayan Akshardham, the complex displays centuries of traditional Indian and Hindu culture, spirituality, and architecture.
The temple covers 32 acres (13 hectares) and is built to look as if is hundreds of years old. It measures 356 ft. long, 316 ft. wide and 141 ft. high, covering an area of 86,342 sq. ft. The main building at the centre of the complex is a 141ft high monument of Bhagwan Swaminarayan.

Mahakal Temple

The Mahakaleshwar Temple in Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh, is one of the most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.
It is known not only for its religious significance but also for its unique and stringent rituals.

Khatu Temple

The Khatu Shyamji Temple is a Hindu Tempe in Khatoo Village at a distance of 43 km from Sikar City, Rajasthan, India.
The temple is a popular Hindu Pilgrimage site where Lord Krishna and Barbarika /Khatushyam are worshipped. Devotees believe the head of Khatushyam sacrificed his head upon the request of Lord Krishna during the war.
